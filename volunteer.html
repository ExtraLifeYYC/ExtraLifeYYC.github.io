An Extra Life Guild is a group of passionate Extra Lifers who want to get more involved and help strengthen the Extra Life community on a local level. The Calgary Guild meets regularly to find ways to help grow the Extra Life movement by registering new Extra Lifers, fundraising for the Alberta Children's Hospital and sharing the Extra Life movement. Guild members will have the opportunity to attend local gaming industry events and conferences, hang out with other Extra Life community members, and meet some of the kiddos treated at our local children's hospital!

We are always welcoming of new volunteers. If you're thinking of volunteering, just fill out the form below, tell us a little about yourself and why you Extra Life. Extra Life Calgary is about having fun first. There is no volunteer work that is too small. ---
